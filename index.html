Australian Bushfires Data Visualisations

This dashboard explores the patterns and impact of Australian bushfires between 2015 and 2023. Along with the air pollution to see if a relationship is present within the two. First, lets look at some visualizations regarding bushfires across Australia. The map (left) shows the geographic distribution of fire incidents across Australian states, while the pie chart (right) highlights the proportions of different fire types per year. Together, these visualisations illustrate how both the location and causes of fires vary annually.

Now lets look at the data of each state. The scatter plot (left) compares the number of fires and total burned area across states and years, while the stacked bar chart (right) illustrates how much each state contributes to Australia's overall air pollution annually.

The graph (left) shows the total burned area of fire incidents across the years, while the graph (right) shows the average air pollution across the years. These visualisations are later merged together to show their correlation.
