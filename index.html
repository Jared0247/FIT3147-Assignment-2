Australian Bushfires Data Visualisations

This dashboard explores the patterns and impact of Australian bushfires between 2015 and 2023. Use the interactive visualisations to explore how fire types, burned areas, air quality, and state-level distributions changed over time.

The map (left) shows the geographic distribution of fire incidents across Australian states, while the pie chart (right) highlights the proportions of different fire types per year. Together, these visualisations illustrate how both the location and causes of fires vary annually.
