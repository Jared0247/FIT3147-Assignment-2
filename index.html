Australian Bushfires Data Visualisations

Interactive Vega-Lite visualisations exploring Australian bushfire data from 2015–2023.

Visualisation 1: Fire Types by Year (Interactive Pie Chart)

This pie chart shows the proportion of different fire types (Prescribed Burn, Bushfire, Unknown) across years from 2015 to 2023. Use the slider to explore year-by-year changes.

Visualisation 2: Total Burned Area Over Time

This area chart shows the total area burned each year from 2015–2023. The year 2019, which had the largest burned area, is annotated.
